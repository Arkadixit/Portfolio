How I Use AI

I learned to code before AI tools became widely available and reliable, which gave me a strong foundation in problem-solving, debugging and system understanding. That said, I fully acknowledge the power of modern AI tools and the productivity gains they enable today.

I do not see progress as something to resist. Instead, I choose to work with it in a thoughtful and controlled way.

I use AI in a thoughtful and controlled way. Generated code is never copied blindly: I review it, adapt it and fully understand it before including it in a project. Without this ownership step, code quickly becomes fragile and hard to maintain. At the same time, AI is an extremely powerful tool, and choosing not to use it would mean ignoring a major productivity gain. The goal is to benefit from its strengths while keeping full control over design decisions, code quality and long-term maintainability.
